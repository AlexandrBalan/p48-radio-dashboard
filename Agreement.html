1. Purpose

This page explains how our team will stay organized, communicate clearly, divide work, and support each other during the UTICA Radio Map project.

2. Team Members and Roles

Alex

Scrum Master / Developer / Support

Leads the team, tracks progress, keeps work organized, and helps with development tasks when needed.

Necobi

Product Owner

Reviews priorities, keeps focus on client needs, and helps guide project direction.

David

Developer

Builds and updates website pages, works with data, and tests features.

Antonio

Developer

Helps develop features, improve the website, and complete sprint tasks.

3. Communication

  • We use iMessage for quick updates.
  • We use Basecamp to manage tasks and project work.
  • Team members should check messages regularly and reply when needed.
  • We will communicate more during future sprints and stay more connected as a team.

4. Meeting Schedule

  • Main meeting: Monday, 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM.
  • Extra meetings will be decided in the group chat.
  • We may meet before presentations or sprint reviews if we need more preparation.
  • We will try to use class time to finish our work first. If we do not finish everything on time, we will use the Monday 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM meeting as a backup meeting.

5. Work Expectations

  • Each member is responsible for their assigned tasks.
  • Everyone should try to complete tasks on time.
  • If someone needs help, they should tell the team early.
  • We will support each other and communicate respectfully.

6. Team Agreement

We agree to communicate clearly, respect each other, complete our assigned work, and support the success of the project.
